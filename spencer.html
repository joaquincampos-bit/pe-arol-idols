Alberto Spencer

One of Peñarol's greatest idols

Alberto Spencer is a Peñarol idol primarily for his impressive goal-scoring ability and the numerous titles he won with the club, including three Copa Libertadores, two Intercontinental Cups, and eight Uruguayan championships. He became the all-time leading scorer in the Copa Libertadores with 54 goals, marking a successful era for the team in the 1960s.
