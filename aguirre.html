Diego Aguirre

One of Peñarol's greatest idols

Diego Aguirre is an idol at Peñarol for his dual role as a player and coach, achieving milestones such as the goal that gave Peñarol its last Copa Libertadores in 1987 and leading the team to multiple league titles, including the Uruguayan championship in 2015. He also stood out as the coach who led the club to its last Copa Libertadores final, demonstrating his connection with the institution at different stages.
