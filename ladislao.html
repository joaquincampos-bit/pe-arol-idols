Ladislao Mazurkiewicz

One of Peñarol's greatest idols

Ladislao Mazurkiewicz is considered a Peñarol idol for his talent as a goalkeeper, his decisive performances, and the titles he won with the club. His achievements include being Uruguayan champion on several occasions (1965, 1967, 1968, and 1981), champion of the Copa Libertadores and the Intercontinental Cup in 1966, and champion of the Intercontinental Champions' Super Cup in 1969.
