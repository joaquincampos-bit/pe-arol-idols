Nestor Goncalves

One of Peñarol's greatest idols

Néstor "Tito" Gonçalves is considered a Peñarol idol for being the player with the most appearances in the club's history, for his numerous titles, and for his unwavering loyalty and love for the club, which led him to spend his entire career at Peñarol. Furthermore, he was a key figure in the "golden decade" of the 1960s, winning the Copa Libertadores and the Intercontinental Cup multiple times.
